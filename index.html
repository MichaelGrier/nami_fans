All About Nami

Hello Humans! My name is Nami Elizabeth Consuela Grier, but you probably know me by my Instagram name: _Nami_Cat. I am a tiny, fluffy and uniquely adorable Korat who makes her home in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. My interests include: naps, eating chicken, playing with springs, and knocking anything I can find onto the ground.

I was born the youngest of 40 cats in the basement of a hoarder's house, so my early life was pretty rough. Eventually though, I was rescued from that nasty place and found my forever home, with two wonderful human servants who attend to my every need. They were so obsessed with me, that they even decided to create an Instagram account to show my fluffiness off to the world!

Recently, my humans have gotten so many requests for more content of yours-truly, that I commanded them to create this website, so that I can provide my fans and fluffy furriends with all of the Nami-goodness that they desire!
